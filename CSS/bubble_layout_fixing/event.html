The Policy Cafe: Public Policy Discussion and Networking

Teach First

4 More London Riverside, London SE1 2AU, United Kingdom

Thursday 16 July 2015 6:30 PM until 8:00 PM

Times are tough in the public sector.  With growing austerity and a rapidly aging population, services are under real strain.    But we know that public sector trainees have the ideas and drive to tackle this challenge head on. That’s why we’re holding an event to share and build ideas between people on public sector graduate schemes. 

Based on the World Cafe concept we are bringing together graduates to discuss key issues facing policymakers and public service users. 

Attendees will join one of 5 tables, which will include representatives from different organisations.  A question will then be posed to the group, which will have 15 minutes to discuss the idea and solutions.  The group will then move on the next table, and the next question. 

At the end, all the groups ideas will be summarised and discussed.

The event provides a real opportunity for great discussion, idea development and networking.

Attendees will includes graduates from the Civil Service Fast Stream, the Local Government Association's NGDP scheme, NHS Graduate Scheme and Teach First.


Space will be limited so please ensure you book your ticket in advance via http://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/the-policy-cafe-public-policy-discussion-and-networking-tickets-17464861872.
